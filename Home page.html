Advanced Technological Institute-Badulla

ATI is one of the leading government institutions in Uva Province, in higher education sector in Sri Lanka which has been established by the parliament act no 29 of 1995, and coming under the purview of the Ministry of City Planning, Water Supply and Higher Education. There are 19 Advanced Technological Institutes operated by SLIATE island wide having conducted a broad range of multi-disciplinary programs targeting at A/L qualified students in Sri Lanka.
